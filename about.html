A little background about

R is a friendly and freely available programming software, that means you or your funders don’t need to pay any money to use this. R can handle almost all kind of data with great visualizations. The built in programs in R is really convenient and there is a huge amount of R packages exist in public domain. Also there are lots of online materials to learn this very famous programming language (just google it).

installation process

The installation process of R and R Studio is very convenient. Please go through online to download R and R Studio . Note that do install R studio only after installing R in your PC. Please choose the latest version of R and R Studio based on your PC and OS specification.
